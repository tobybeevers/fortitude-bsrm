# Fortitude Bespoke Solutions & Risk Management

## Discreet Security. Intelligent Risk Management.

### Overview

Fortitude Bespoke Solutions & Risk Management is a Dubai-based consultancy delivering high-level security advisory and risk management solutions to high-net-worth individuals, family offices, and corporate leaders at a global level.

We specialise in discreet, intelligence-led strategies designed to protect people, assets, and reputations in an increasingly complex global environment.

### Founder

Founded by a UK-qualified Close Protection professional with over 13 years of operational experience, including long-term protection of a senior member of the Saudi Royal Family.

The founder holds a Security Manager certification from the Security Industry Regulatory Agency (SIRA) in Dubai, ensuring full compliance with UAE regulatory standards and best practices.
