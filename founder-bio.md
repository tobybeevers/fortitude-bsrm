# Personal Bio

## Founder & CEO

I am a Dubai-based Security & Risk Consultant and Founder of Fortitude Bespoke Solutions & Risk Management, specialising in discreet protection strategies for high-net-worth individuals, family offices, and corporate executives.

### Experience

I bring over **13 years of licensed close protection experience** under the UK SIA framework, including long-term responsibility for the protection of a senior member of the Saudi Royal Family. This experience has provided extensive exposure to complex threat environments, international travel security, and the delivery of low-profile, high-impact protection solutions.

My background also includes **experience within British Special Forces environments**, contributing to an advanced understanding of operational security, risk mitigation, and intelligence-led planning.

### Credentials

I hold the **Security Manager certification** from the Security Industry Regulatory Agency (SIRA), ensuring full alignment with UAE regulatory standards and best practices.

### Philosophy

My approach is centred on **discretion, prevention, and precision**—delivering bespoke security and risk management solutions tailored to each client's lifestyle, exposure, and operational requirements.
