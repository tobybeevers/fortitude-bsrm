About the Founder

Experience forged at
the highest levels.

Fortitude was established by a Dubai-based security professional whose career has been shaped by the most demanding operational environments available to a close protection practitioner. Over thirteen years of SIA-licensed experience, including a long-term appointment as lead protection officer for a senior member of the Saudi Royal Family, have produced a depth of expertise that is genuinely rare in this field.

That background encompasses complex international travel security, multi-layered threat environments, and the kind of sustained, high-stakes responsibility that cannot be replicated through training alone. It has also forged an instinct for discretion — an understanding that the most effective security is the kind that remains invisible to everyone except those who need to know it exists.

Additional experience within British Special Forces environments contributes a rigorous, intelligence-led approach to planning and risk mitigation. Every engagement begins with analysis, not assumption.

Holding the Security Manager certification from the Security Industry Regulatory Agency (SIRA), Fortitude operates in full alignment with UAE regulatory standards while applying the international best practices of UK-qualified close protection. Through a trusted global network of security professionals and intelligence resources, we deliver seamless protection wherever our clients operate.

Every client engagement is handled personally by the founder. When you engage Fortitude, you have his complete and undivided attention.
